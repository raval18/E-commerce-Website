WELCOME TO UPASANA METALS

Upasana Metals proudly welcomes you to the world where quality and flawless service are prime objectives. This motto has made us the leading trader of industrial grade ferrous and non-ferrous metals.

OUR HISTROY

The company was originated in 1999 in Ahmedabad by Ms. Urvashi Shah and right from its inception the company has firmly believed that gender or geographical boundaries do not limit the growth of human progress. Now we are one of the most reliable sources of Industrial grade metals covering the entire Western Region including the coastal areas.

In 2004 Upasana Metals branched out a new company called Karuna Metals as an executive unit for reformed metal products. This company is dealing with cable trays, wire mesh, perforated sheets etc. of various metals. As the member of Upasana Metals, the company brings synergistic strength & experience of years in the metal trading industry.
